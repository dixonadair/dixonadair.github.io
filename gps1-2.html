What is pair programming?

Pair programming is when two people programming together on the same code, and in which one person takes the navigator role and the other takes the driver role.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

Pair programming is important because two minds are always better than one. Two people can share feedback with each other and give tips that the other person might not know about.

Compare driver and navigator

The driver is the person who is typing, or implementing, the code, while the navigator is the one giving instructions.
